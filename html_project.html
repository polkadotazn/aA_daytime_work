Noodle Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp. sriracha
  • 1/2 inch fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 jalape√±o optional
  • 1/4 cup water or more as needed
  • tofu diced

Dressing Ingredients

  • 6 oz. dry soba noodles
  • 1/2 purple cabbage diced in small pieces
  • 1 large carrot julienned
  • 6 small sweet peppers julienned
  • 1 green onion diced
  • 1/4 - 1/2 cup cilantro minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped unsalted peanuts

Soba Noodles Dressed in Peanut Sauce

Description

Soba noodles originate from Japan. They are made out of buckwheat, and contain all eight essential amino acids. They can be eaten in a variety of different ways. I love this recipe because it is cool and refreshing and has an overwhelming peanut flavor! This recipe is not for peanut-haters.

Cooking Directions

  1. FOR THE DRESSING: Add all of the ingredients to a blender or food processor. Add water as needed until desired consistency is reached. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed. Cover, and place in the fridge until ready to use.
  2. FOR THE SALAD: Cook soba noodles according to package instructions. After draining, run noodles under cold water. Dry off with paper towels and place in a large bowl. Add remaining salad ingredients and toss to combine. Add dressing to taste and enjoy!
